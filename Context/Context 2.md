# Context 2 - Momentus Technologies Reference

## Overview

**Momentus Technologies** (formerly Ungerboeck) is a leading venue and event management software provider founded in 1985. Rebranded in January 2023, it serves over 60,000 users across more than 57 countries.

---

## Target Market

- Convention and exhibition centers
- Stadiums and arenas
- Performing arts centers
- Universities and corporate campuses
- Conference centers
- Casino resorts and hotels
- Fairgrounds
- Government and municipal facilities

---

## Product Tiers

- **Momentus Enterprise** — Large-scale venues (convention centers, universities, corporate campuses)
- **Momentus Elite** — Stadiums, arenas, performing arts centers, and conference centers
- **Momentus Prime** — Small and midsize businesses (SMBs), simplified workflows with enterprise-grade capabilities
- **Momentus WeTrack** — Risk and incident management, operations, and sustainability tracking

---

## Five Primary Operational Modules

1. **Sales & Booking** — Lead management from inquiry through contract, proposals with e-signatures, online payments, real-time calendar synchronization to prevent double bookings.
2. **Event Management** — Unified event details, timelines, and layouts with real-time team and client collaboration and automated task management.
3. **Event Operations** — Mobile-first tools for work orders, inventory tracking, digital checklists with photo documentation, and instant status updates.
4. **Accounting** — Flexible payment schedules, invoicing linked to deliveries, automated collections, quote-to-payment tracking.
5. **Dashboards & Reports** — Real-time KPIs, space utilization analytics, customizable dashboards.

---

## Additional Capabilities (35+ integrated modules)

- **CRM & Sales** — Sales funnel, customer accounts, RFP handling, contracts, email management
- **Event Planning & Client Portal** — Document sharing, schedules, task review for staff and customers
- **Catering Management** — Digital booking, menu management, delivery operations with integrated accounting
- **Space Booking & Calendar** — Real-time availability, shared calendars, approval workflows, gap identification
- **Exhibitor & Vendor Management** — Applications, contracts, booth placement, registration, judging, payouts
- **Room Diagramming** — Visual space planning
- **Sustainability Tracking** — Environmental impact monitoring
- **API** — Third-party system integrations
